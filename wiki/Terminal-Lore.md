# 📟 TERMINAL LORE: THE MAGI SYSTEM

Historical and contextual background of the marsianin206 Central Command interface.

## 🏢 Origin: NERV HQ
This interface is a tactical clone of the terminals used in the **Geofront** below Tokyo-3. It was designed by Akagi Naoko and maintained by the technical division to provide pilots with a familiar environment during remote operations.

## 🖥️ The MAGI Supercomputer
The backend of this terminal is theoretically powered by the three Magi sub-systems:
1. **MELCHIOR-1**: Represents the scientist perspective. Handles code integrity and logic.
2. **BALTHASAR-2**: Represents the mother perspective. Handles user empathy and pilot dossiers.
3. **CASPER-3**: Represents the woman perspective. Handles design aesthetics and easter eggs.

## 📅 The Year 2026
Following the revised timeline, 2026 marks the year of significant technological advancement in the **Human Instrumentality Project**. This terminal serves as a communication node for the Final Battle.

## 📜 Key Terminology
- **LCL**: Link Connect Liquid. The orange fluid used for pilot synchronization.
- **Sync Ratio**: The degree of neural overlap between the pilot and the Eva unit.
- **Pattern Blue**: Biological signature of an Angel.
- **Geofront**: The underground fortress housing NERV HQ.
